What it is

Training, skills and pathways around connectivity, geospatial data and climate services.

Key capabilities

  • Bootcamps for data and EO skills with practical project work.
  • On-the-job mentoring and coaching options for teams.
  • Partner-friendly formats for universities, hubs and programmes.

Who it’s for

  • Municipal and government upskilling programmes.
  • University, TVET and innovation hub partnerships.
  • Donor-funded workforce and youth initiatives.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
