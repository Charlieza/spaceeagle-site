EagleSAS

Location and device APIs for secure geolocation and geospatial context in your own platforms.

What it is

Location and device APIs for secure geolocation and geospatial context in your own platforms.

Key capabilities

  • Secure device geolocation SDKs ready for mobile and web.
  • Telematics and asset tracking support with flexible data models.
  • Usage-based billing and multi-tenant support for platform builders.

Who it’s for

  • Fleet and asset tracking solution providers.
  • Fintech and insure-tech platforms needing location services.
  • IoT platforms that require spatial context and mapping.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
