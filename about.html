About SpaceEagle

SpaceEagle delivers future-ready satellite connectivity and Earth-observation intelligence across Africa. Our mission is to empower communities, strengthen enterprises, and support governments through next-generation digital infrastructure.

Our Vision

We imagine a connected Africa where every individual, business, and institution has access to broadband and decision-ready intelligence, no matter how remote.

Our Work

  • Satellite broadband for homes, enterprises, and rural communities
  • Geospatial intelligence for agriculture, climate, and urban planning
  • Sovereign-grade EO systems for governments
  • Specialized training and capacity development

Leadership

SpaceEagle is supported by directors with deep experience in astronomy, satellite missions, Earth observation, data science, and development impact. Our leadership focus is on delivering meaningful, practical solutions for real-world challenges across Africa.
