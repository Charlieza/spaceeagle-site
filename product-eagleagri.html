EagleAgri

Farm and rangeland insights from space for farmers, co-ops, insurers and agri-finance.

What it is

Farm and rangeland insights from space for farmers, co-ops, insurers and agri-finance.

Key capabilities

  • Crop health indices and stress alerts aligned to African crops.
  • Rangeland condition and grazing pressure indicators.
  • Field-level or regional analysis options depending on context.

Who it’s for

  • Advisory services for smallholder and emerging farmers.
  • Input financing and credit risk assessment for agri portfolios.
  • Index-based insurance product design and support.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
