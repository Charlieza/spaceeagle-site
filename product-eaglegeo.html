EagleGeo

Geospatial analytics as a service for land cover, change detection and infrastructure mapping.

What it is

Geospatial analytics as a service for land cover, change detection and infrastructure mapping.

Key capabilities

  • Land cover and land use classifications at multiple scales.
  • Change detection for vegetation, water and built-up areas.
  • REST APIs and web dashboards for teams and partners.

Who it’s for

  • Environmental monitoring and reporting programmes.
  • Infrastructure inventory and asset management.
  • Baseline mapping for development and investment projects.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
