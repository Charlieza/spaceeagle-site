EagleClimate

Climate indicators and decision support tailored to local planners and project teams.

What it is

Climate indicators and decision support tailored to local planners and project teams.

Key capabilities

  • Rainfall and temperature anomaly tracking over key regions.
  • Drought and heat stress indices aligned to local thresholds.
  • Reporting support for climate-aligned projects and proposals.

Who it’s for

  • Local climate risk and vulnerability assessments.
  • Screening of project pipelines for climate risk and opportunity.
  • Support for adaptation proposals and funding bids.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
