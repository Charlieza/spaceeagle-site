EagleNet

Satellite broadband across RSA & SADC, designed to plug into real-world last-mile realities.

What it is

Satellite broadband across RSA & SADC, designed to plug into real-world last-mile realities.

Key capabilities

  • High-throughput satellite links suitable for home, SME and community-scale nodes.
  • Integration with existing ISP and municipal networks, with flexible hand-off options.
  • Managed service model available, including monitoring and basic SLAs for key sites.

Who it’s for

  • Rural schools and clinics that need dependable internet access for education and health services.
  • Municipalities piloting Wi-Fi zones, smart villages, or connected community hubs.
  • Enterprises and hubs requiring backup or primary links where fibre is not yet viable.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
