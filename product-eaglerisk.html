EagleRisk

Disaster risk and early-warning tools built on satellite-derived hazard layers.

What it is

Disaster risk and early-warning tools built on satellite-derived hazard layers.

Key capabilities

  • Historical risk layers and exposure mapping for key hazards.
  • Near-real-time monitoring streams for floods, fires and drought.
  • Simple visual dashboards usable by non-specialists.

Who it’s for

  • Municipal disaster management centres and provincial teams.
  • Insurance portfolio risk analysis and pricing support.
  • Resilience planning and adaptation projects in vulnerable areas.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
