EagleVillage

Community Wi-Fi and shared access nodes designed for villages, townships and peri-urban areas.

What it is

Community Wi-Fi and shared access nodes designed for villages, townships and peri-urban areas.

Key capabilities

  • Solar-ready outdoor Wi-Fi hotspots linking to a satellite or fibre backhaul.
  • Voucher, pre-paid and sponsored access models supported out of the box.
  • Configurable for schools, clinics, libraries and community hubs in one footprint.

Who it’s for

  • Multi-site school and clinic networks in one district.
  • Community Wi-Fi in townships and rural growth nodes.
  • Public–private pilot projects with municipalities and innovators.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
