EagleSovereign

Space data sovereignty advisory for governments, agencies and regional initiatives.

What it is

Space data sovereignty advisory for governments, agencies and regional initiatives.

Key capabilities

  • Data policy and governance roadmaps for EO and space-derived data.
  • Archiving, cataloguing and access strategies for national and regional hubs.
  • Capability and standards planning aligned with global best practice.

Who it’s for

  • National space and Earth observation programmes.
  • Regional data hubs and multi-country initiatives.
  • Donor-funded capacity-building and infrastructure projects.

Implementation notes

Implementation details are tailored to partners, hardware choices and country context. SpaceEagle typically co-designs deployments with local technical teams, implementers and institutions.

For pilots or programmes, SpaceEagle can host a joint architecture session to align product choices, data flows and operational models.
